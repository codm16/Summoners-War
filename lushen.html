Summoners War

Lushen

lushen is one of the best monsters available in the game. He is a great Hydeni and Aiden Hell farmer. He can also do very well in the arena when paired with another Lushen or AoE damage dealer. The biggest thing about him is his AoE ignore defense. A well runed Lushen + AoE team can clear most arena defense in 15 seconds below Conqueror 1.
