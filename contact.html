Guild Wars

During the siege, attacking members have to set up 2 teams of 3 monsters. You will be fighting two 3 vs 3 battles against other guilds. When war is declared on a guild, the attacking member can fight 3 times. You cannot attack the same defender twice.

When you win 1 round, 30% of the enemyâ€™s guild HP is lost. Any subsequent win will drop some more, but at a -5%(25%, 20%, 15%) until 10%. A draw will cause the enemy guild to lose 5% HP. During a siege, the guild members with higher ratings will be on the left side while the ones with lower rating will be on the right. Winning against higher rated guild members will give you more guild points.
